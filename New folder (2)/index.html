Frequently Asked Questions →
  • Q: Who is eligible to vote in India?
    A: Every Indian citizen aged 18 or above on the qualifying date.
  • Q: How can I register as a new voter?
    A: You can fill Form 6 online after login to register as a new voter.
  • Q: How can I update my voter details?
    A: You can update details by filling Form 8 online.
  • Q: How do I check my voter ID status?
    A: You can use the “Track Application Status” option in the Services section.
  • Q: What should I do if my name is not in the voter list?
    A: Reapply using Form 6 or contact your BLO for assistance.
